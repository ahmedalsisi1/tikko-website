Tikko Token - Roadmap

Phase 1: Foundation

  • Creation of the Tikko Token smart contract
  • Launch of official website
  • Setup of social media channels
  • Initial marketing campaign

Phase 2: Community Building

  • Building a strong and engaged community
  • Giveaways and token airdrops
  • Telegram and Discord growth
  • Influencer collaborations

Phase 3: Ecosystem Expansion

  • Listing on decentralized exchanges (DEX)
  • CMC & CoinGecko listing applications
  • Launch of staking platform
  • Website multi-language support

Phase 4: Future Development

  • Partnerships with DeFi platforms
  • Integration with wallets and services
  • Utility development (NFTs, GameFi, etc.)
  • Full DAO governance implementation
